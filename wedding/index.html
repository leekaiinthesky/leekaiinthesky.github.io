Activities and attractions within walking distance of hotel

The Museum of Contemporary Art (next door)
The Broad Museum (1 block)
The Walt Disney Concert Hall (1 block)

Other suggested attractions in L.A.

Getty Center and Villa – museums, sculptures, outdoor gardens
LACMA and La Brea Tar Pits – contemporary art and adjacent archeological site
Runyon Canyon – rigorous hiking with a panoramic view of the city
Griffith Park – hiking with a view of the Hollywood sign, downtown L.A., Griffith Observatory
L.A. Live – Staples Center, Grammy Museum, restaurants, bowling
The Grove and Farmer's Market – open-air shopping center with food stalls
Santa Monica Pier – beach and boardwalk
Abbot Kinney and Venice Beach – trendy cafes, boutiques, galleries
Venice Canal Historic District – built in 1905, modeled after Venice, Italy
