Getting In and Staying Here

Omni Hotel

To book a room, contact Elaine Morales, our Conference Services Manager, at (213) 356-4008, and mention our wedding. Our rate is no longer available via their website or front desk, but it is available through Elaine.

We are staying at Omni Los Angeles Hotel at California Plaza and have reserved a block rate of $209/night. It is walking distance to our welcome dinner, and Sunday's brunch will be at the hotel. We have a room quota, so please consider staying here with us!

Getting there: http://www.omnihotels.com/hotels/los-angeles-california-plaza/property-details/directions (approximately $46 by taxi from Los Angeles International Airport). Our group has a parking rate of $38/day with in-and-out privileges.

Activities and attractions within walking distance of hotel

Grand Central Market (historic food and drink market, 0.4 miles)
The Museum of Contemporary Art (next door)
The Broad Museum (1 block)
The Walt Disney Concert Hall (1 block)
L.A. Conservancy Walking Tours (some tours leave from Pershing Square, 0.3 miles)
The Standard Hotel and Rooftop Bar (0.6 miles)

Other suggested attractions in L.A.

Getty Center and Villa – museums, sculptures, outdoor gardens
LACMA and La Brea Tar Pits – contemporary art and adjacent archeological site
Runyon Canyon – rigorous hiking with a panoramic view of the city
Griffith Park – hiking with a view of the Hollywood sign, downtown L.A., Griffith Observatory
L.A. Live – Staples Center, Grammy Museum, restaurants, bowling
The Grove and Farmer's Market – open-air shopping center with food stalls
Santa Monica Pier – beach and boardwalk
Abbot Kinney and Venice Beach – trendy cafes, boutiques, galleries
Venice Canal Historic District – built in 1905, modeled after Venice, Italy
